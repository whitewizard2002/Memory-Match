How to play
At the beginning of the game, all the cards are mixed up and laid in rows, face down on the screen.
One by one you have to find the correct match for each card that is on the screen.However there are certain cards that work differently than normal cards, their description is mentioned below.
You need to finish the game in alloted time.
If you choose to quit the game halfway through than no score will be provided.

Special Cards

When two of the Clock cards are found, they will increase the time limit of the game

When found, you can use it with any other card to find its pair

When The Eye is used , it will flip all the cards for a certain period of time
